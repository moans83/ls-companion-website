Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Insert date]

LS Companion is designed to help you track symptoms, self-checks, and patterns related to lichen sclerosus in a simple and private way.

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible while still providing meaningful insights.

1. Information You Provide

You may choose to enter information such as:

  • Symptoms
  • Self-check entries
  • Notes
  • Triggers and care activities

2. Account Information (Optional)

You can use LS Companion without creating an account.

If you choose to sign in using Apple, we may store a unique identifier and (optionally) your email address if you choose to share it.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud services).

If you are signed in, your data is associated with your account so it can be accessed across devices.

If you are not signed in, your data may only be stored locally on your device.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide symptom tracking and insights
  • Help you identify patterns over time
  • Improve the app experience

5. Use of Anonymized Data for Insights

To generate summaries and insights, some data may be processed using AI-based services.

This processing is done using data that is anonymized and designed not to identify you personally.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We use trusted third-party services (such as Firebase) to securely store and process data on our behalf.

7. Your Choices

  • Use the app without signing in
  • Delete the app at any time
  • Request deletion of your account data

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

LS Companion is not intended for children under 13.
